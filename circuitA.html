Circuit Connection :

  1. Connect one leg of the LDR to the 5V pin on the Arduino.
  2. Connect the other leg of the LDR to analog pin A0 on the Arduino.
  3. Connect one end of a resistor (10kÎ©) to the same leg of the LDR and connect the other end to the GND pin on the Arduino.
  4. Connect the control pin of the relay module to digital pin 7 on the Arduino.
  5. Connect the input side of the relay module to the 5V pin and GND pin on the Arduino.
  6. Connect the output side of the relay module to the LED strip or light source.
